About Us

TechShiksha'17 is a program by Sanshiksha Academy in association with Pi Space to promote technical innovation in schools.

Techshiksha event is an overall competition with students coming up with creative and innovative designs while winning prizes by proving their mettle in the workshop. Best Teams from Round 1 of every school will be selected for Free of Cost workshop.

Problem Statement(Round 1):

With the inclusive development in this changing era, there have been many unfamiliar structural,commercial, and social problems that make affect our lives drastically. But with the combination of Innovative ideas and technology we can solve these problems effectively and efficiently. Some of these problems come from the areas such pollution( Air, Water, Soil) , agriculture, defence and security, disaster management, transportation facilitation, health and medical issues etc. For the Aavishkar Challenge, students will have to suggest simple, effective, and feasible solution to solve problems near your locality and environment. There is no word limit for your answer report but it is mandatory to make a 2D/3D drawing of your design labelling different parts and explaining various functions of each parts.

Selection Procedure: Each submission will be judged on the basis of the creativity and feasibility of the design. Judges will expect a clearly explained design and with proper labelled diagram. 1 or 2 teams (Each team consisting 5 students ) from each school will get selected for the Aeromodelling workshop in the next round of the competition. Round 2: A workshop on RC Aircraft will be organised for selected teams from all the schools in round 2. Details about the same are given in Workshop section. Results: Results will be announced at the end of December on Techshiksha website and facebook page.

Seminar

(Introduction to Aeromodelling)

Students will be given a taste of aerospace engineering and the current industry developments. This lecture will give them basic knowledge of
  • History of aviation.
  • Different types of aerial vehicles (planes, heli, rocket, satellite,etc).
  • Basic physics of an airplane.
  • Functioning of various components of airplane.
  • Current scenario of various Aviation Vehicles.
  • Control of an airplane.
  • Where does the future lie in Aviation?
